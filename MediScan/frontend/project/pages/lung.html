About Lung Diseases

Lung diseases are some of the most common medical conditions worldwide. Early detection through imaging can significantly improve treatment outcomes for many respiratory conditions.

Common Lung Conditions

  • Tuberculosis: Infectious disease that primarily affects the lungs
  • Pneumonia: Infection that inflames air sacs in one or both lungs
  • COPD: Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease
  • Lung Cancer: Abnormal cell growth in lung tissue
  • Pulmonary Edema: Fluid accumulation in the lungs
  • Pleural Effusion: Excess fluid between the layers of the pleura

Warning Signs

  • Persistent cough (especially with blood)
  • Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  • Chest pain or discomfort
  • Wheezing or abnormal breathing sounds
  • Chronic fatigue
  • Recurring respiratory infections
  • Unexplained weight loss

Prevention Tips

  • Don't smoke or quit smoking
  • Avoid exposure to pollutants
  • Prevent respiratory infections
  • Get vaccinated (flu, pneumonia, COVID-19)
  • Exercise regularly to strengthen lungs
  • Practice good hygiene
  • Get regular check-ups
