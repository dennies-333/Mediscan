About Skin Diseases

Skin diseases encompass a wide range of conditions that affect the skin, including rashes, inflammations, infections, and growths. Early detection is crucial for effective treatment.

Common Skin Conditions

  • Melanoma: A serious form of skin cancer
  • Eczema: Inflammation causing itchy, red, and rough skin
  • Psoriasis: Condition causing red, itchy, scaly patches
  • Acne: Skin condition with pimples and spots
  • Rosacea: Condition causing facial redness and visible blood vessels
  • Dermatitis: Skin inflammation with itchy, red rash

Warning Signs

For moles and skin growths, remember the ABCDE rule:

  • Asymmetry: One half doesn't match the other
  • Border: Irregular, ragged, or blurred edges
  • Color: Varied colors within the same mole
  • Diameter: Larger than 6mm (pencil eraser)
  • Evolving: Changes in size, shape, or color

Prevention Tips

  • Use sunscreen with SPF 30+ daily
  • Wear protective clothing in the sun
  • Avoid tanning beds
  • Perform regular skin self-examinations
  • Stay hydrated and maintain a healthy diet
  • See a dermatologist annually for skin checks
