About Bone Conditions

Bone and joint conditions affect millions of people worldwide. Early detection through imaging can help guide appropriate treatment and prevent further complications.

Common Bone Conditions

  • Fractures: Breaks in bone continuity
  • Osteoarthritis: Degeneration of joint cartilage and underlying bone
  • Rheumatoid Arthritis: Autoimmune disorder affecting joints
  • Osteoporosis: Decreased bone density and strength
  • Bone Tumors: Abnormal growths in bone tissue
  • Joint Dislocations: Displacement of bones at joints

Warning Signs

  • Persistent joint pain or stiffness
  • Swelling or redness around joints
  • Limited range of motion
  • Unusual sounds (clicking, popping) from joints
  • Visible deformities
  • Unexplained bone pain, especially at night
  • Fractures from minor injuries

Prevention Tips

  • Maintain a calcium-rich diet
  • Get adequate vitamin D
  • Exercise regularly (weight-bearing and strength training)
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol
  • Prevent falls and injuries
  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Get bone density screenings as recommended
